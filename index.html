Chloe & Euan's 10th wedding anniverary

Also celebrating Eric's 3rd birthday

It's great that you can join us to celebrate 10 years of our marriage and Eric's third birthday.*

We will be celebrating from 12:30pm till 6pm on 3rd September at Risinghurst Community Centre.

Timings

Feel free to drop in as you're able and stay as long as you like. However, there are a few timings that you may want to bear in mind…

2pm – food

We'll have snacks and drinks out as long as they last, but are planning to serve food (hog-roast style rolls for meaties, chickpea curry for veggies) from 2pm.

2:30pm – cake cutting

Before the kids arrive for Eric's party we plan to cut the cake and say a few words, so if you can be present for that part of the day it would be great.

3pm-5pm – Eric's party

We suggest that if you're coming along primarily to celebrate Eric's birthday you aim for this period of the afternoon. There will be Octonauts themed fun!

There is plenty of outdoor space for children to run around, so if you want to bring any outdoor games, they'll come in handy (weather permitting!).

Getting there

The party will be at Risinghurst Community Centre

By car

There is a small car park at the Community Centre, but ample, free on-street parking is available on the surrounding roads.

By bus

The number 9 bus runs every half hour from the city centre and stops very close to the venue.

Park & ride

If you're coming from London on the bus, Thornhill Park & Ride is a 15-20 min walk from the venue.

Gifts

We don't require gifts for us or Eric – seeing you will be present enough. We have, however, been let down by the caterers so will be cooking all the food ourselves. If you fancy bringing along a salad or similar, please do let us know.

If you can't make it

If you're no longer able to make it, we'll miss you! Please drop us a line to help with logistics.
